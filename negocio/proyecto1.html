The origin of Dolce Platino

"Dolce Palatino Handmade Ice Cream" was born in 2016 in Madrid. Its Venezuelan Italian founder, who is a food lover, decides to start creating a concept of ice cream based on daily own elaboration with the best products on the market to create an unmistakable flavor and texture.

100% handmade
All our products are made in our devices respecting the passion for "artisanal", that unique flavor that sets it apart from a mass-produced product.
Natural ingredients
We have the best ingredients, high quality raw material and with designation of origin.
More than 20 flavors
It has more than 20 flavors of handmade ice cream, among which are: Chocolate, Oreo, dark chocolate, lemon pie.
Daily elaboration
We know how important it is that our customers enjoy the best ice cream, that's why our fundamental rule is to make the ice cream daily.
Freshness and flavor
With our daily elaboration process, we can guarantee maximum freshness and the most genuine flavor that characterize all our ice cream.
More than ice cream
You will find delicious smoothies, smoothies, slushies, horchata, meringue milk and coffees. And you can not miss our handmade pastry and hot chocolate.
